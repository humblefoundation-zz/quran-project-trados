* aYN = āyah number, without leading zeros

* aRABIC_TEXT_WITHOUT_TASHKEEL = the text of the aayah, without tashkeel marks
* tAFSEER_SADI_ARABIC = the tafseer of the aayah from as-sa'di
\end{comment}
